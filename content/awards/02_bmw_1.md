### Project Overview
Digital platform for BMW Group employees covering brand behavior guidelines and strategic content. Consistent experience across smartphones, tablets, and desktops, with distinct visual identities for BMW and MINI.

### Challenge
BMW Group needed to standardize brand behavior knowledge across the organization with a secure, accessible tool that could communicate complex brand strategies while keeping employees engaged.

### My Role
As **Lead Full-Stack Developer and UI Designer**, I built the core 3D rendering engine using WebGL and Three.js (extending my bachelor thesis research on interactive 3D PWAs), designed the gamified learning interface, and implemented the responsive frontend. I also developed backend services for content management and user progress tracking.

### Solution
A progressive web app with multilingual brand strategy content, gamified learning modules, editorial content on brand evolution, a visual timeline of brand heritage (including MINI's journey from 1959 to today), and customer-focused sections. The responsive design reflects BMW and MINI's distinct visual identities.

**Tech:** Vue, WebGL, Three.js, Node.js, REST APIs, Progressive Web App
