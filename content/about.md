# I'm Valérie

![alt](../blob_about.png)

\- a machine learning specialist and full-stack developer. My background spans software engineering, web-based 2D and 3D applications, and AI research. I’ve spent the last decade working on projects at the intersection of complex technical problems and real-world application.

I hold an M.Sc. in Informatics from TUM, where I focused on Machine Learning and Computer Vision. For my thesis, I developed AI-based surrogate models for automotive simulations at BMW Group. Before that, I spent eight years designing and implementing cross-platform solutions in educational technology. My expertise lies in 3D geometry applications, computer vision, and generative AI architectures.

> "I combine technical depth with creative curiosity, always exploring where code meets creativity."

Beyond my professional work, I'm an avid maker and creator. You might find me building and testing experimental FPV drones, reconstructing digital landscapes from drone footage, drafting sewing patterns, or preparing authentic Asian and Bavarian dishes. I also organize an international illustration meetup, connecting creative minds across borders.
